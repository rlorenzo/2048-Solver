What We Collect

This app runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your game state (board, moves, seed) is stored only in your browser's URL bar for sharing purposes and is never sent to our servers.

We use Google AdSense to display a single ad unit on the page. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

This app does not set any first-party cookies. Third-party cookies may be set by Google AdSense for advertising purposes. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Third-Party Services
